Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Our specialized financial extraction engine is trained specifically on bank layouts. All processing occurs in-memory in your browser, ensuring no financial data is ever transmitted to our servers.

Is there a free version?

Yes! Use our Guest tier for 3 free conversions. Registered free users get 10, while our Pro plan unlocks unlimited orchestration and large file support (up to 50MB).

What formats are supported?

We primarily support Digital PDF bank statements and convert them into Microsoft Excel (.xlsx). We focus on digital files to preserve 100% accuracy for audit trails.

Who uses QuickConvert?

We serve auditors, forensic accountants, fractional CFOs, and business owners who need to normalize heterogeneous bank data into clean accounting ledgers.

Is the extraction accurate?

Yes. Unlike generic OCR tools, our engine is deterministic. It understands bank-specific logic like credits, debits, and running balances to ensure zero reconciliation errors.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Absolutely. You can manage your Pro subscription through our secure billing portal. No hidden fees or long-term contracts; cancel with one click whenever you need.
