Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Cookie Disclosure: We use essential and performance cookies to provide a secure and efficient financial ETL experience.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you and remember your preferences.

2. Essential Cookies

These are required for the platform to function. They allow us to manage your login session and secure your account through Supabase.

3. Performance & Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about how the platform is used. This helps us optimize processing speeds and user experience.

4. Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

Google use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

5. Preference Cookies

These remember your settings, such as which tool you used last, to provide a seamless "one-click" workflow experience.

6. Controlling Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging into your account or accessing "Pro" features.
